QR-Code Generator

QR-Code Generator and Scanner, allows users to generate QR codes from text inputs or URLs. Additionally, it offers a scanner functionality using the device's camera to decode QR codes in real-time, providing the decoded text or URL. This tool is useful for creating and scanning QR codes quickly and efficiently, supporting various use cases such as sharing URLs, contact information, or other text-based data in a convenient digital format.
