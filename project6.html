Projects Website

The website incorporates a nostalgic 80s style design, evoking the aesthetic of that era through its choice of colors, fonts, and graphics. The color palette prominently features vibrant neon hues such as bright pink and electric blue, reminiscent of the bold and energetic colors popular in 80s graphic design. The use of geometric shapes and gradients further enhances this retro vibe, echoing the visual language often seen in 80s arcade games and music videos. Typography plays a crucial role with bold, blocky fonts that are characteristic of the era, adding to the overall nostalgic feel. This design approach not only pays homage to the 80s aesthetic but also creates a distinctive and engaging user experience that resonates with fans of retro culture.
